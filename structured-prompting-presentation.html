WHY "DIRECT" PROMPTS FAIL

The "Statistical Leap" vs "Stepping Stones"

‚ùå DIRECT PROMPT
"What's wrong and how to fix it?"
‚ùì
Generic theory dump

Long leap = High risk of hallucination

‚úì CHAIN-OF-THOUGHT
"Let's think step-by-step..."
Step 1: What's missing?
Step 2: Where did it break?
Step 3: Smallest fix?
Precise diagnosis

Short steps = High accuracy

KEY INSIGHT: LLMs don't have a "brain" to think privately. They must "think out loud" on screen to stay on track. Each written step becomes context for the next.
